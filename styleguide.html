Basic Branding Ideas

What does the company do, or offer?

Our company mainly offers stationery based on paper products, such as notebooks, planner, etc. We also provides pencils, book clips and other affiliate products. Our company's value proposition is to produce simple, environmentally friendly and useful products with high quality, discard fussy, fancy or redundant designs, and at the same time, deliver a relaxed and comfortable atmosphere to our customers.

Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)?

The customer segments our company mainly target are those aged 18 - 35, who have concern about environmental protection. Also, for those who get tired of dazzling and complicated design elements in the market, and pursue some simple, practical, and high-quality products, our company will be a good choice.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

One of the features of our company is that we use recycled paper to create all paper products. In most of the recycled paper, paper fibers are often lost in the washing process that separates ink from paper. So as you can see, our paper's brightness fades and presents the slightly yellowish hue. However, there is nothing difference from regular paper in the sense of experience. We are committed to finding out the most environmentally friendly materials to protect our customers and our environment. In addition, we actively involve our customers in the design process and help us create products that satisfy with their need.
